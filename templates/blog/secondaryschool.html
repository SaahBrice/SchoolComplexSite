The Best Bilingual College In Dschang And Cameroon as a Whole

Rainbow Bilingual College programme encourages initiative, supports individualised academic growth, and helps our students build character, confidence, and cross-cultural sensitivity.

Our college is divided into two sections: the junior section and Senior section where the curriculum focuses on preparing students for GCE Ordinary level and Advance Level respectively, in a broad range of subjects.

What makes our college unique:

Core curricular subjects are supported with a variety of supplementary subjects that provide our students with excellent academic and personal growth opportunities.

Learning and campus life are enriched by our General Topic, "Discovery".

Our project-based learning approach enhances skills through student-driven projects that emphasise teamwork, problem solving, and reflection.

Students can take part in cross-cultural learning exchange within their peers across the RAIBICOL Education Network.
