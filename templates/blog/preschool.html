The Best Bilingual Nursery School In Dschang And Cameroon as a Whole

As a parent, you always want what’s best for your child. Ensuring that your most loved ones will have a secure and positive childhood experience is often huge deciding factor in moving homes- whether that be from one neighborhood to the next, or to another country.

Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School has become an incredibly attractive school for foreigners and Cameroonian families, as the the school offers one of the most top notch foundation for kids. In fact, Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School is amongst some of the highest ranked Nursery schools in Cameroon.

Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School is an important first step school in a child’s education and can set the foundation for a future of academic success. But on a more subtle level, we take early age education as a critical milestone in a child’s social and emotional development hence under the guidance of our values, we strive towards academic excellence at all levels.


For many children, it’s their first time entering a social environment without the close support of their parents. Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School gives the child the opportunity to gain autonomy, socialize and collaborate with peers, and construct a sense of self identity and confidence.

For expatriate families in particular, it's critical to foster these developmental attributes, as expat children are more likely to experience a sense of isolation and otherness. Entering a child into a supportive learning environment at a young age can help them feel more integrated and adapted as a “third culture kid.”

Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School promotes language and communication skills. Many children grow up in bilingual households and speak French and English in the home than they do on the playground. While this can provide huge benefits to the child later in life, it’s common that these children have slight delays in linguistic development as a child. Rainbow Bilingual Kindergarden School is a great place for a child to develop their language and communication skills and prepare them for primary school.
